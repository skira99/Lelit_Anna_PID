# Lelit_Anna_PID
Lelit Anna 2 PL41LEM Modifications

The Lelit Anna single boiler espresso machine comes in three models: PL41EM, PL41LEM, and PL41TEM. Only the "TEM" model comes with a PID from the factory, whereas the "EM" and "LEM" do not (the only difference between those two are the buttons being converted to flick switches in the "LEM" model). As there is a commercially available model of the Anna with PID, there are no third party kits (such as the popular Auber kits for the Gaggia classic or the Rancilio Silvia). Owning a PL41LEM and not being satisfied with the performance of the machine, I quickly began researching the possibility of installing a PID in my machine.

After a few years of use, the pump in my machine started to stall, leading to the first iteration of work:
-Understand hydraulic and electric path
-Install PID
-Change pump
-Fix issues with pressure gauge fluttering during shots
